Discussion Questions: Week 7

Use these questions to guide your thinking before and during class discussion.

Good Will Hunting

1. BELIEF

Why does Will believe he doesn't deserve success or love? How do early experiences shape our core beliefs about ourselves?

2. PURPOSE

Sean tells Will, "It's not your fault." Why is this simple statement so transformative? What makes therapeutic breakthroughs possible?

3. UNCERTAINTY

Will pushes away everyone who gets close. What function does this self-sabotage serve psychologically?

4. MOTIVATION

What finally motivates Will to change? Is it Sean's therapy, Skylar's love, or something internal?
