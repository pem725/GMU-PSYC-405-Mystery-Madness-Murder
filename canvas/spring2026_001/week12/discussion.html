Discussion Questions: Week 12

Use these questions to guide your thinking before and during class discussion.

The Hurricane

1. PURPOSE

Rubin Carter maintains his innocence for decades despite enormous pressure. What psychological resources allow someone to maintain their sense of self under such circumstances?

2. BELIEF

How did racial bias and confirmation bias contribute to Carter's wrongful conviction? What cognitive errors led investigators astray?

3. DISCOMFORT

Carter refuses to wear prison clothes or participate in prison programs. What psychological function does this defiance serve?

4. MOTIVATION

What motivated Lesra and the Canadians to fight for Carter's freedom? What does their commitment reveal about altruism and justice-seeking behavior?
