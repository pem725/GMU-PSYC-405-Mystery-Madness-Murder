Discussion Questions: Week 5

Use these questions to guide your thinking before and during class discussion.

Fight Club

1. BELIEF

The narrator creates Tyler Durden to embody everything he wishes he could be. What does Tyler represent about suppressed aspects of identity?

2. DISCOMFORT

The film critiques consumerism and modern masculinity. What psychological needs does the narrator try to meet through material possessions, and why do they fail?

3. MOTIVATION

What drives the members of Fight Club and later Project Mayhem? Is their violence a symptom or an attempted cure?

4. EMOTION

The narrator's dissociation is triggered by chronic insomnia and alienation. What does modern research tell us about the relationship between sleep deprivation and psychosis?
